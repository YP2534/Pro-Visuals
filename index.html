Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers.

How can drone photography benefit my real estate listing?
In a competitive market, an aerial view is a game changer. Drone photography provides a unique perspective that ground-level shots simply can't match. It allows potential buyers to see the entire property layout, the neighborhood, and proximity to landmarks.
Do you provide drone services for weddings?
Absolutely! Nothing adds "cinematic scale" to a wedding film like a sweeping aerial shot of the venue. Our wedding packages are designed to give your love story a grand, movie-like feel.
What happens to the raw footage after the shoot?
Unlike many others, we believe the data belongs to you. In most of our packages, we provide all raw video footage alongside the final edited files, giving you the freedom to use the clips for different social media edits.
Is it legal to fly drones at my location?
Safety and legality are our top priorities. We stay updated on local regulations. While we handle the technical side, we do require permission from the property owner. For certain restricted zones, we may need to check feasibility beforehand.
